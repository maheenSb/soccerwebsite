Jefferson Cup 2026

The Jefferson Cup is one of the largest and most prestigious youth soccer tournaments in the United States, held every March in Richmond, Virginia. It attracts teams from across the country in boys and girls divisions.

Tournament Description

The tournament features hundreds to thousands of youth teams competing in different age groups and divisions each year. The event includes both Championship and Showcase formats, offering competitive match play and opportunities to be seen by college coaches and scouts.

Format of Play

Younger teams (U9–U15) typically play a championship format, while older teams (U16–U19) play in showcase divisions to maximize exposure and player development.

Showcase & College Exposure

Showcase weekends provide opportunities for college scouts and coaches to evaluate top players, while also including high-level matches for spectators.
