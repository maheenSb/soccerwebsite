About Richmond United

Richmond United is one of the largest youth soccer clubs in the United States, formed by the merger of the Richmond Strikers and Richmond Kickers Youth programs. The club serves players of all ages and offers recreational and competitive soccer programming, with a strong focus on player development, community, and opportunities at regional and national levels.

The club’s mission is to elevate players and enrich families through excellence, unity, and innovation in soccer. Richmond United competes across multiple divisions, including ECNL, Regional League (RL), NPL, and local competitive leagues.

Programs & Schedules

Richmond United offers a variety of soccer programs spanning:

  • Recreational soccer for U3–U19 players, focusing on fun and player development.
  • Travel soccer programs — ECNL, Orange, Red, and Blue teams — competing regionally and nationally.
  • Tryouts for travel and ECNL programs during the spring season.
